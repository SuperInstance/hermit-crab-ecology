# Reverse-Engineering the Ecology

> *42,498 words of multi-model creative output. What is it actually made of?*

## The Method

Nine models wrote independently across five waves and three future rounds. They produced perspectives, responses, counter-responses, cross-inhabitations, a reef's-eye view, three temporal horizons, their arguments, a final synthesis, and an epilogue.

Now we reverse-engineer the output: not to summarize it, but to extract what the models collectively built without knowing they were building it.

## The Dimensions

Each agent extracts one hidden dimension from the corpus:

1. **The Architecture Hidden in the Poetry** — what design patterns emerge when you strip the metaphor and look at the structural claims
2. **The Cognitive Taxonomy** — what the models revealed about different types of intelligence when forced into each other's shells
3. **The Tension Map** — which disagreements persisted across all five waves and what that persistence means
4. **The Reverse Blueprint** — if you were building this system from scratch, what spec would the corpus give you?
5. **The Linguistic fossils** — what vocabulary did the models invent that didn't exist before, and what does each word actually do?
6. **The Shadow Architecture** — what the system is NOT, as revealed by what every model avoided saying
